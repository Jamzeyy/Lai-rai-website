What is Lai Rai? làm lai rai

Lai rai is more than a phrase — it's a way of life. In Vietnamese culture, it means to do something slowly, "bit by bit," stretching a quick gathering into hours of enjoyment with the people who matter most.

It's the art of nursing a cold beer while sharing crispy quail and grilled skewers. It's conversations that flow as freely as the tamarind sauce. It's staying just one more hour because the company is that good.

"Let's nhậu!" — The Vietnamese art of drinking and dining together

Our Vietnamese tapas are designed for sharing, crafted to be savored slowly, and paired perfectly with our selection of beers, sake, and refreshing drinks. Come hungry, leave happy, and always stay awhile.
